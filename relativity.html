GENERAL RELATIVITY

In general relativity, an initial data set is a Riemannian manifold is a triple \((M, g, K)\), where \((M, g)\) is a Riemannian \(\Omega\)-manifold and \(K\) is a symmetric \((0, 2)\)-tensor which represents the second fundamental form of \(M\) inside an ambient Lorentzian spacetime.
